About the platform

Built for placement cells, by placement workflows

Smart Placement Exam Portal brings the entire placement examination lifecycle — question banking, scheduling, secure delivery, evaluation and analytics — into a single, focused system so placement teams spend less time on logistics and more time on outcomes.

  • Role-based Admin and Student portals
  • Automatic evaluation with negative marking support
  • Exam integrity deterrence: full-screen, tab-switch, copy/paste block
  • Placement readiness scoring across every attempt
